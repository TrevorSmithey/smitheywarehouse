Design Notes

Tier 1 (Primary)

  • Product, Hobson, Selery, Total, DOI
  • Full opacity, bold values
  • These are the operational metrics

Tier 2 (Secondary)

  • Velocity (sky-400/40), Open Orders (purple-400/40)
  • 40% opacity, lighter weight
  • Dashed separator line
  • Planning/forecasting data
